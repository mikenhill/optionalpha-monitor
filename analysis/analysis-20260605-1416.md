## Teaching Point Triggered

### Negative Gamma Environment With 7550 Pin/Magnet Potential

Today's setup maps most closely to a **negative gamma amplifying environment** with a **potential GEX pin at 7550**.

The key evidence:
- Net GEX is deeply negative at -13.14B — **amplification risk is high**
- Sentiment at 55% — slight positive gamma majority, but not enough to offset the massive negative drag
- 7550 has record two-sided exposure — call GEX +2.59B, put GEX -3.64B, call OI 4,459, put OI **6,267**
- Price is 34 points above 7550 — room to move but 7550 is an extremely powerful gravitational centre
- The concentration at 7550 (6.24B) is the second highest in the sample
- Put OI at 7550 is the highest ever seen — major put positioning

This is a **dangerous but high-probability setup**: the massive two-sided concentration at 7550 creates strong pin potential, but the deeply negative net gamma means that if 7550 breaks, the move could accelerate violently in either direction.

- **The put side dominates at 7550** (put GEX -3.64B vs call GEX +2.59B), suggesting the strike may act as a **support pillar** on the way down.
- **However, the negative net gamma environment means support is not guaranteed** — if 7550 breaks with momentum, the deeply negative GEX could cause acceleration rather than rebound.
- **The call OI-weighted mean at 7620.66** suggests a potential **call wall / resistance zone near 7600-7620** if price rallies.
