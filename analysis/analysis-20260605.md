## Teaching Point Triggered

### Positive Gamma Environment With 7550 Pin/Magnet Potential

Today's setup maps most closely to a **positive gamma stabilising environment** with a **potential GEX pin at 7550**.

The key evidence:
- Net GEX is positive (+1.15B) — stabilising/amplifying-dampening environment
- Sentiment at 62.5% — majority of strikes are positive gamma
- 7550 has heavy two-sided exposure — call GEX +2.08B, put GEX -2.70B, call OI 3,946, put OI 5,114
- Price is 34 points above 7550 — room to move but 7550 is a gravitational centre
- Call/put weighted means bracket the key strike with bullish skew above

However, the put side is slightly heavier at 7550 (more put GEX and significantly more put OI), so it is not a clean symmetric pin. It leans toward a **positive gamma environment with 7550 as a support pillar that also has pin characteristics**.

### Practical Read

- **7550 is the immediate floor** — price is 34 points above it. Any pullback toward 7550 would test a major two-sided level.
- **The call-skewed weighted means** (call centre above spot, put centre below) suggest upside is less resisted than downside is supported.

- **Breaking below 7550** would be significant because put exposure is substantial there. A clean break would put price into the put-weighted zone around 7543-7545.
- **The wider OI and volume spreads** (54.83 and 65.90) suggest call flow is concentrated further above spot — potential for continued upside drift if momentum persists.
