## Teaching Point Triggered

### Negative Gamma With 7525 Pivot / Potential Slide Risk Below

Today's setup maps most closely to **negative gamma acceleration risk**, with a possible **GEX slide** if SPX loses 7525 decisively.

It is not a clean GEX pin because call and put exposure are not balanced enough and net GEX is negative. It is not a clean GEX wall because the key strike is not call-dominated. It is only a partial GEX pillar because 7525 has more put GEX and put OI than calls, and same-day volume at the key strike is also put-heavy.

### Practical Read

- **7525 is the immediate pivot** — price is 28.68 points above it, giving more breathing room than the earlier 7550 reading.
- **Holding above 7525** is currently the case by a wide margin, but any approach toward 7525 would test the key level.
- **Breaking below 7525** would put price into the area where put exposure is centered lower, particularly the GEX/OI weighted area around 7533-7539 and the volume-weighted area near 7511.
- **The stronger downside reference is the lower put-weighted zone** around 7533 and potentially 7510 if selling accelerates.
